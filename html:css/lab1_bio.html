Bellatrix Lestrange (1951 â€“ 2 May, 1998) was an English pure-blood Dark witch, the eldest daughter of Cygnus and Druella Rosier, and the elder sister of Andromeda Tonks and Narcissa Malfoy. She was a member of the House of Black, an old wizarding family and one of the Sacred Twenty-Eight. Bellatrix started her education at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the early sixties (either 1962 or 1963), and was Sorted into Slytherin House.

Bellatrix Lestrange's Victims

  • Frank and Alice Longbottom: torturing them by means of the Cruciatus Curse in her attempt at forcing them to yield the information she wanted. The event rendered the Aurors clinically and permanently insane, therefore condemning them to St Mungo's Hospital for Magical Maladies and Injuries for the remainder of their lives.
  • Sirius Black: "blood traitor" relative. Murdered during the Battle of the Department of Mysteries
  • Nymphadora Tonks: her niece who was "pruning her family tree". Tonks is half-blood, an Auror, member of the order of the Phoenix, and married a werewolf. Murdered during the Battle of Hogwarts
  • Dobby: murdered for saving Hermione
  • Hermione: despised her for being Muggle-born and selected her to be tortured in order the truth out of her as to why the trio had Godric Gryffindor's sword.
